## Component Annotations

### Artist Profile Card
- **Surface:** Soft Violet glass `#EDE5F5` @ 15%, blur 16px, border-radius 16px
- **Avatar:** Circular, 64px desktop / 48px mobile, artist photo or illustrated self-portrait
- **Label:** "Illustrated by" вҖ” Space Grotesk 500 / 12px / uppercase / Label Brown `#876749`
- **Artist name:** Space Grotesk 500 / 17px desktop, 16px mobile / Obsidian `#1A1A1A`
- **Style description:** Inter 400 / 17px desktop, 16px mobile / Warm Charcoal `#2C2A26` вҖ” 1-2 sentences max describing illustration style
- **Preview thumbnails:** 3 mini images of their designs, square, border-radius 8px, arranged in a row
- **Design count:** "X designs" вҖ” Caption / 14px / Clay Earth `#816A4F`
- **CTA:** "View Portfolio вҶ’" вҖ” Ghost button, Stone border, Obsidian text
- **Hover (desktop):** Card lifts, avatar gains 2px ring in Dusk Violet `#6B4C8A`
- **Click:** Entire card links to `/artists/[slug]`

### Grid Layout
- **Desktop:** 3 columns, 24px gap
- **Tablet (768px):** 2 columns
- **Mobile (< 640px):** Single column, full-width cards
- **No pagination needed** вҖ” artist count expected to be small (< 20)

### Artist Role Framing
- Artists are "enablers" вҖ” they forge the tools the hero (customer) needs
- Craft is visible; personal identity is secondary to the work
- "Illustrated by" framing (not "Created by" or "Designed by")
- Never position artist as the main character
