| COD Operations Protocol  NEW V1.7  — COD is 35–98% of Egypt e-commerce. This is not a payment method; it is the primary business model. |  |  |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Pre-Dispatch (Reduce Fake Orders)** 1\. Every COD order gets a WhatsApp confirmation message within 2 hrs: item, size, area, total, expected delivery window. 2\. Customer must reply "confirmed" or equivalent before dispatch. No reply after 12 hrs → follow-up. No reply after 24 hrs → cancel and note. 3\. For orders \>900 EGP, call to confirm (30-sec script: verify name, area, item). 4\. Prepaid incentive: offer free shipping for card/wallet payment. Never frame COD as a problem — frame prepaid as a reward. | **During Delivery** 1\. Send tracking link via WhatsApp on dispatch day. 2\. Day before expected delivery: "Your HORO order arrives tomorrow — someone will be there to receive?" 3\. If courier reports failed attempt: contact customer within 1 hr to reschedule. Max 2 attempts. 4\. Courier instructions: handle package carefully, be polite. Courier experience \= brand experience. | **Post-Delivery & RTO** 1\. Delivery confirmed → send thank-you WhatsApp \+ UGC request after 48 hrs. 2\. RTO (returned to origin): log reason. If customer unreachable → move to rejected list. If area/timing issue → offer re-delivery once. 3\. Target metrics: RTO \< 8%, COD confirmation rate \> 90%, prepaid share \> 30% by month 6\. 4\. Weekly review: RTO by area, by courier, by price tier. Act on patterns, not anecdotes. |
